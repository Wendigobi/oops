🎃 Gotcha! 🎃

You clicked a link that *looked* like YouTube… but it wasn’t!
Don’t worry — this one is just for fun. 👻

This is how **phishing works**: hackers dress up malicious links in disguises.
(Kinda like Halloween costumes 🎭)

⚠️ How Hackers Trick You:

  • Spooky “urgent” messages 👀
  • Fake login pages that *look* real 🕵️
  • Disguised links like the one you just clicked 🕸️

🛡️ How to Stay Safe:

  • Hover over links before you click.
  • Double-check the sender’s email.
  • Never share your password — even with IT!
